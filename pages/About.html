About Us

Welcome to our website. We're a group of everyday people committed to making the internet a safer place by helping identify and report phishing websites. Our mission is to protect others from falling victim to online scams through awareness and collective effort. We might not be cybersecurity experts, but we are dedicated to learning and sharing what we know to help others stay safe online. Our project started in 2024 with a simple goal: to make the web safer for everyone. Our core values are honesty, vigilance, and community. We believe that anyone can make a difference, and together, we can fight against phishing attacks and keep the internet secure.
